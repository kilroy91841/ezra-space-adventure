# Ezra's Space Adventure

A magic robot's journey through space to defeat the dragon!

## Play the Game
Open `game.html` in your browser.

## How to Play

### Controls
- **Arrow Keys** - Move your robot around
- **SPACEBAR** - Shoot (hold for rapid fire!)
- **1, 2, 3, 4** - Switch between weapons

### Weapons (Ezra's Favorites!)
1. **Ice Shooter** - Fast ice projectiles
2. **Fire Shooter** - Spreads fire in 3 directions
3. **Giant Sword** - Huge melee attack
4. **Big Ginormous Bomb** - Massive explosion!

### Game Flow
1. **Dimension 1: Power-Up Hunt** - Explore and find hidden power-ups in special places
2. **Dimension 2: Alien Boss** - Defeat the alien (easiest boss)
3. **Dimension 3: Mini Dragon** - Practice for the final battle
4. **Dimension 4: DRAGON BOSS** - Epic final showdown!

### Tips
- Collect power-ups to make your weapons stronger
- Bosses DROP power-ups when defeated - collect them!
- More power-ups = more damage
- Dodge enemy projectiles to keep your power-ups
- The Dragon gets TOUGHER in Phase 2 (under 50% health)

## Architecture
- `src/engine/` - Core game loop and state management
- `src/entities/` - Game characters (robot, bosses, power-ups)
- `src/weapons/` - Weapon system with Ezra's favorites
- `src/dimensions/` - Collection and boss battle dimensions

## Run Tests
Open individual test files in `tests/` directories.
