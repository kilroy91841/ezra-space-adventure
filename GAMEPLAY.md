# Ezra's Space Adventure - Gameplay Guide

## Quick Start

1. Open `game.html` in your web browser
2. Press SPACEBAR on the title screen to start
3. Use Arrow Keys to move your magic robot
4. Hold SPACEBAR for rapid-fire shooting!

## Weapons

Press number keys to switch weapons:

### 1. Ice Shooter (Fast!)
- **Type:** Shooter
- **Damage:** 7 + power-ups
- **Speed:** Super fast projectiles
- **Best for:** Quick attacks, keeping distance

### 2. Fire Shooter (Spreading!)
- **Type:** Shooter
- **Damage:** 8 + power-ups
- **Speed:** Shoots 3 fireballs in a spread
- **Best for:** Hitting multiple targets, area coverage

### 3. Giant Sword (HUGE!)
- **Type:** Melee
- **Damage:** 20 + power-ups (POWERFUL!)
- **Range:** Close range, massive hitbox
- **Best for:** High damage when close to bosses

### 4. Big Ginormous Bomb (BOOM!)
- **Type:** Summon
- **Damage:** 25 + power-ups (MASSIVE!)
- **Effect:** Floats up then EXPLODES in huge area
- **Best for:** Maximum damage, hitting hard

## Dimensions

### Dimension 1: Power-Up Hunt
- **Goal:** Explore and find hidden power-ups
- **Tips:**
  - Check all 4 corners (hidden power-ups!)
  - Look around the edges
  - Yellow stars with ? marks are hidden ones
  - Collect at least 60% to progress

### Dimension 2: Alien Boss
- **Boss Health:** 30 HP (Easiest)
- **Attack Pattern:** Slow green projectiles
- **Strategy:**
  - Dodge green blobs
  - Use Ice Shooter for fast attacks
  - Stay moving!
  - Boss drops 5 power-ups when defeated

### Dimension 3: Mini Dragon
- **Boss Health:** 50 HP (Medium)
- **Attack Pattern:** Single fireball attacks
- **Strategy:**
  - Faster than alien
  - Use Fire Shooter for spread damage
  - Keep distance and dodge
  - Boss drops 4 power-ups

### Dimension 4: DRAGON BOSS (Final Battle!)
- **Boss Health:** 100 HP (REALLY TOUGH!)
- **Attack Pattern:**
  - Phase 1: Two fireballs at angles
  - Phase 2 (under 50% HP): THREE fireballs, faster!
- **Strategy:**
  - Use Giant Sword when close for big damage
  - Use Big Bomb for massive damage
  - Stay in center, dodge left and right
  - Phase 2 is INTENSE - focus on dodging!
  - Boss drops 10 power-ups when defeated!

## Power-Up System

- **Collection:** Yellow stars scattered through dimensions
- **Effect:** Each power-up adds +1 damage to ALL weapons
- **Loss:** Getting hit by enemy projectiles removes 1 power-up
- **Boss Drops:** All bosses drop power-ups when defeated!

### Power-Up Math
With 10 power-ups:
- Ice Shooter: 17 damage per shot
- Fire Shooter: 18 damage per shot (x3 projectiles!)
- Giant Sword: 30 damage per swing
- Big Bomb: 35 damage explosion!

## Pro Tips

1. **Rapid Fire!** - Hold spacebar for continuous shooting (5 frame cooldown)
2. **Stay Moving** - Always keep moving to dodge enemy projectiles
3. **Collect Boss Drops** - Don't forget to grab power-ups after defeating bosses!
4. **Weapon Switching** - Switch between weapons based on situation
5. **Hidden Power-Ups** - Dimension 1 has many hidden ones in corners
6. **Dragon Phase 2** - When dragon hits 50% health, it gets MUCH faster and angrier!
7. **Save Bombs** - Big Bomb is great for Dragon Phase 2

## Scoring

- Hit boss: +10 points
- Collect power-up: +50 points
- Higher score = more accurate shooting!

## Victory Conditions

Defeat the Dragon Boss to win! Your final score and power-up count will be displayed.
